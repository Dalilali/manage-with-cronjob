Welcome to Webtool CHISEL


This webtool provides a convenient platform for managing and automating tasks on your server. With its user-friendly interface, you can easily create, manage, delete, update, activate, and deactivate cron jobs to ensure that your important tasks are executed on time.

Principally it use only Cron to setup the jobs
To define the time you can provide concrete values for
minute, hour, day of month (day), month,
and day of week (dow) or use '*' in these fields (for 'any')

┌───────────── minute (0 - 59)
│ ┌───────────── hour (0 - 23)
│ │ ┌───────────── (day) day of the month (1 - 31)
│ │ │ ┌───────────── month (1 - 12)
│ │ │ │ ┌───────────── day of the week (0 - 6) (Sunday to Saturday)
│ │ │ │ │
│ │ │ │ │

* * * * * command

In addition to cron jobs, you can also create non-scheduled jobs that can be executed manually at any time.
